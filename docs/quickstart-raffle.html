Raffle App Mobile Setup

Add the UORC Raffle app to your phone or tablet home screen for faster access on run day.

Quick Setup

Select your device below and follow the install steps.

Install on iPhone or iPad

Safari is required. Do not use Chrome on iPhone or iPad for this setup.

Open the raffle app in Safari, then add it to your home screen.

Open Raffle App

After it opens in Safari, tap Share, then tap Add to Home Screen.

Already installed. This app is currently running from the home screen.

  1. Open the raffle app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button. The Share button is the square with the upward arrow at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Rename it to UORC Raffle if you want.
  5. Tap Add.

You will now have an app icon on your home screen.

It should open like a normal app with minimal browser chrome.

Install on Android

Open the raffle app and install it to your home screen.

Open Raffle App

If your browser supports app install, the Install App button will appear automatically.

Already installed. This app is currently running from the home screen.

  1. Open the raffle app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app.
  4. Confirm the name as UORC Raffle.
  5. Tap Install or Add.

The app icon will appear on your home screen.

It should open full-screen like an app.
